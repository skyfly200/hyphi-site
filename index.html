Origin

Light as a living
medium

Hyphi grew from a deep obsession with light, motion, and the festival floor. Every product starts as a maker experiment — boards laid out by hand, firmware written from scratch — and only ships when it earns its place in the world.

We design for flow artists, festival-goers, and anyone who believes their space deserves something stranger and more beautiful than a standard lamp. Our hardware is open-source. Our ethos is community.

Based in Boulder, CO. Assembled with care. Lit with intent.

Introducing

Glow Flora

An organic LED sculpture that turns any surface into a living light source. Battery-powered and endlessly programmable — from a single tabletop bloom to an installation of hundreds, all in sync.

Illumination

48 Individually Addressable LEDs

Six silicone petals, each carrying a flex WS2812B strip with 8 LEDs. Two independent channels let you mirror, chase, or counterpoint patterns across any petal pair.

LED type WS2812B
Total LEDs 48 · 6 petals × 8
Channels 2 × 24 LEDs
Sync OWLS · Wi-Fi · BLE
Design

Organic by Design

Custom-molded silicone petals diffuse light with a soft, living quality. The poseable anodized stem holds any angle. A capacitive touch ring on the base controls brightness — no app required.

Height 28 cm
Petals Translucent silicone · IP44
Stem Anodized aluminium · poseable
Control Capacitive touch ring
Inside

Every Part Has a Purpose

Designed to be repaired, not replaced. Every component is documented, replaceable, and available as a spare. Because good light deserves a long life.

Open Hardware & Firmware

Schematics, firmware, LED position data, and 3D models are published under MIT license. Use them to build your own, extend the ecosystem, or just inspect what's inside. The OWLS sync protocol is an open standard — any hardware can implement it.
